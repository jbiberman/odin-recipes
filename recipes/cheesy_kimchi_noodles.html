Cheesy Kimchi Noodles

Description

Instant ramen, mixed with melted cheese and kimchi. Balanced out with scallions, garlic, Sriracha, and soy sauce, with an egg on top, it's the ideal substitute to a night out on a cold evening.

Ingredients

For the noodles:

  • 6 ounces dried instant noodles
  • 1.5 tbsp canola oil, plus more for coating cooked noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 scallions, diagonally sliced (reserve 1 tbsp for garnish
  • .5 cup packed kimchi, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds, plus more for garnish
  • .5 cup grated Cheddar

For the sauce:

  • 2 tbsp Sriracha
  • 1-2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp Shaoxing wine

Steps

  1. Bring a medium saucepan of water to boil over high. Add noodles and cook according to package instructions until tender but still springy, about 2-3 minutes. Transfer to a colander and rinse in cold water until cool, then toss with a splash of canola oil to stop from sticking together. Set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce: whisk the Sriracha, soy sauce, sesame oil and Shaoxing wine together in a small bowl and set aside.
  3. Set a medioum nonstick skillet over high heat. Add .5 tbsp canola oil and crack in the eggs once shimmering. Reduce heat to medium and cook until whites are done and edges are browned, 2-3 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Add remaining 1 tbsp canola to same skillet over medium-high. Add scallions, kimchi, garlic, sesame seeds, cook 1 minute until just softened, stirring frequently. Add the noodles and sauce, toss to combine for one minute. Stir in the grated cheese until it melts. Turn off the heat.
  5. Transfer the noodles to serving plates, top w/ fried eggs and garnish with extra sesame seeds and scallions. Serve immediately.
